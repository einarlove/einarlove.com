This was a prototype for a game concept done as a school project in Game Design.

With these four levels in the prototype, I was able to experiment with the game mechanics—like wall jumping and traps—to make interesting level designs, and how to teach the mechanics in a cognitive and experimental manner.

My partner was Sara Nilsdóttir Tomter, who was part of the idea creation and process, and stood for most of the graphics.
